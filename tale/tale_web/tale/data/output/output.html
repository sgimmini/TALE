In the bustling city of Essen, located in the southern part of the Ruhr area, our story begins. This city is divided into two distinct regions, with the better cities and domes in the south and the cheaper, more run-down areas in the north. The population is dense, with the majority of people living underground due to the history of mining in the area. However, life underground is not without its dangers, as tunnel collapses, floods, and gas eruptions are common occurrences, especially during the rainy seasons. To navigate between the domes, people rely on vacuum trains called Tubes, which are categorized into different classes. These Tubes are the lifeline of the city, transporting people from one dome to another. The city is also home to a significant number of YiQiRen (Ais), as well as rats, cockroaches, and iClone animals. The environment is heavily polluted, and food production relies on iClone Food Print technology. Mushrooms are considered a luxury item, and vegetables are only available once a week for the upper class. Access to the Ornithoper, a mysterious location, is regulated through a deep exhaust shaft. Our protagonist, Chrom, lives in a humble camping wagon, sleeping with a VR headset and burnt hair. But his life takes an unexpected turn when he encounters Adelle, a biker on a mission to find her missing sister, Nugget. Adelle is accompanied by her biker gang, known as the "Snakes on Wheels." Desperate to find Nugget, Adelle offers her bike as a reward for anyone who can locate her sister. Chrom, determined to help, starts investigating and discovers footage of Nugget on the cameras. It seems she went out with other children from the trailer park but never returned. It's a common practice for kids to scavenge for trash in Ruhrtal at night. Inspired by this lead, Chrom comes up with the idea of attaching a tracking chip to someone's drone to aid in the search.

In the process, however, Chrom accidentally damages the flood gate, leaving it permanently open. As a storm intensifies, Chrom braves the elements and ventures into the scrapyard, where he finally finds Nugget. But their reunion is not without danger, as they encounter two menacing scrapwolves in a cave. Meanwhile, in another part of the city called DaSun, we meet Eagle Passant, a character who frequents a bar owned by TempestTech. The atmosphere in DaSun is hot and suffocating, with dark alleys and flickering neon lights. Eagle, who suffers from dust-filled lungs, enters a door under a bright neon sign that reads "Die Karibik: Eine DaSun experience brought to you by Tempest Tech." Stepping through the door, Eagle is transported to a beautiful beach, complete with white sand and azure water. Children play ball, and iClone-WaterDwellers hover near the ceiling, creating a surreal atmosphere. The beach is dotted with bar islands, attracting guests from all walks of life. However, a fence separates the higher-class area from the "Economy" section, and signs warn about the risk of drowning, with Tempest Tech not being liable for any incidents. In Die Karibik, Eagle encounters Augusta, a character wearing a nun outfit and high heels. Augusta asks Eagle to secretly race against Samuel Sanchez Gonzalez, a newcomer to MilPool who previously worked at CrisisInvest. There is a rivalry between Augusta and Gonzalez, and Augusta wants Eagle to prove herself by defeating him in a race. The vehicles of choice are a BMW T5 for Eagle and a KMW-Stier for Gonzalez.

The next morning, Eagle and Augusta set off, and the race against Gonzalez begins. Eagle showcases her skill and agility, maneuvering around Gonzalez like a matador in a bullfight. Her tactics cause Gonzalez to crash into a pillar, securing Eagle's victory. In a small alley filled with capsule hotels, we meet Bitwise, a small and unkempt individual. Bitwise lives in one of these capsule hotels and hides Neuralink clinics as headphones. Bitwise is deeply connected to the Matrix and yearns to reach the Fog of War, a place of absolute anonymity. However, unrest stirs in the Fog of War, with rumors of all exits being monitored. The Kis, a group advocating for anonymity, attempt to enter but are unsuccessful. They argue for their right to privacy, staging a demonstration against the Red Star and ID, the entities preventing true anonymity.

As our story unfolds, we see the intertwining lives of Chrom, Adelle, Nugget, Eagle, Augusta, and Bitwise in this gritty and dangerous city of Essen. Each character faces their own challenges and adventures, navigating the treacherous tunnels, polluted environment, and the ever-present dangers of a city on the edge.
